# Cosmology

This repository is a growing collection of notes, reports, and lecture material related to **cosmology and theoretical physics**.\
It is meant to serve as both a personal study archive and a reference for core ideas connecting General Relativity, field theory, and the large-scale structure of the universe.

## Contents

Currently, the repository includes:

- 📄 **Actions in SR, E&M and GR**\
  Foundations of special relativity, electromagnetism in covariant form, General Relativity, curvature, Einstein–Hilbert action, and scalar field theory in curved spacetime.

- 🌌 **Cosmology (Based on Susskind’s Theoretical Minimum)**\
  Lecture-style notes covering:

  - FLRW metric and expanding universe
  - Friedmann equations
  - Matter, radiation, and vacuum energy
  - Dark matter and observational cosmology
  - CMB, thermal history, and inflation

- 🌑 **Dark Matter Discovery**\
  A historical and physical study of:

  - Fritz Zwicky’s virial theorem analysis
  - Vera Rubin’s galaxy rotation curves
  - The emergence of the dark matter paradigm

## Purpose

This repository is intended to:

- Organize cosmology-related study material in one place
- Build a clear bridge between GR, field theory, and cosmology
- Serve as a foundation for deeper work in early universe physics and structure formation
- Grow into a complete personal cosmology notebook



## Note

This is an evolving repository. Files may be refined, expanded, and reorganized as understanding deepens.

Cosmology is where geometry learns to breathe.
